Problem Statement

Target metric

One clear metric to measure impact:

Faster onboarding by 3x

One-liner: Streamlining onboarding for university students by automating manual workflows.

Who: University students, faculty, and admin staff.

What: Current onboarding systems are slow, confusing, and lack automation. We close this gap by building a unified, automated onboarding tool.

Why now: Universities are rapidly digitizing, and students expect seamless digital experiences.

Constraints (judges care):

  • Deployable MVP in ~48 hours
  • Reproducible demo (no flaky infra)
  • Clear impact metrics

Pain points

  • Existing tools are slow to set up
  • Hard to reproduce for judges
  • Lack of automation & testing
